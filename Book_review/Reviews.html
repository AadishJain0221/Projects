Top Picks

Overview the Genres

Fantasy

Harry Potter and the Philosopher’s Stone

A captivating start to the Harry Potter series, this magical tale blends adventure, mystery, and friendship as Harry discovers he’s a wizard and faces the dark wizard Voldemort. A timeless classic for readers of all ages.

The Lord of the Rings

Tolkien’s masterpiece follows the quest to destroy the One Ring and save Middle-earth. Filled with rich lore, complex characters, and epic battles, it’s a cornerstone of modern fantasy literature.

The Hobbit

A delightful prequel to The Lord of the Rings, this adventurous tale follows Bilbo Baggins as he encounters dragons, trolls, and the powerful One Ring.

The Name of the Wind

A lyrical tale of Kvothe, a gifted boy turned legend, recounting his rise through hardship, magic, and mystery. Rothfuss’s writing makes this a mesmerizing fantasy.

Eragon

A coming-of-age tale about a farm boy who discovers he’s destined to ride a dragon and fight an oppressive empire. Perfect for fans of classic fantasy.

Mystery Thrillers

Gone Girl

A psychological thriller that explores the dark complexities of marriage with shocking twists. Flynn’s sharp writing and gripping plot keep readers hooked.

The Da Vinci Code

A gripping thriller combining art, history, and cryptography. Follow Robert Langdon as he deciphers ancient codes and uncovers secrets that could alter Christianity’s foundations.

The Silent Patient

A chilling psychological thriller about a woman who stops speaking after committing a murder, and the therapist determined to uncover her secrets.

The Woman in the Window

A gripping psychological thriller about an agoraphobic woman who believes she’s witnessed a crime, weaving suspense and twists till the end.

Before I Go to Sleep

A chilling psychological tale of a woman with amnesia who uncovers shocking truths about her past with each passing day.

Personal Devlopment

Atomic Habits

A practical guide to making small, consistent changes that lead to remarkable results. Clear provides actionable strategies backed by science for building better habits.

How to Win Friends and Influence People

A classic guide to improving communication, building relationships, and becoming more likable, with timeless principles for personal and professional success.

Grit

An inspiring exploration of passion and perseverance, showing how grit, rather than talent, leads to long-term success.

Daring Greatly

A powerful book about embracing vulnerability, cultivating courage, and living authentically to build meaningful connections and personal fulfillment.

Mindset

A groundbreaking book on the power of adopting a growth mindset to unlock potential and achieve success in all areas of life.

Biographies and Autobiographies

Becoming

An inspiring memoir tracing Michelle Obama’s journey from her Chicago roots to the White House, filled with wisdom, resilience, and empowering life lessons.

Steve Jobs

A revealing biography of Apple’s visionary co-founder, exploring his innovative genius, leadership style, and personal complexities.

Long Walk to Freedom

An extraordinary autobiography chronicling Mandela’s fight against apartheid and his journey to becoming South Africa’s first Black president.

The Wright Brothers

A captivating account of the Wright brothers’ journey to inventing the airplane, celebrating their ingenuity and determination.

My Experiments with Truth

A reflective autobiography detailing Gandhi’s journey of self-discovery, truth, and his philosophy of nonviolence that changed the course of history.
